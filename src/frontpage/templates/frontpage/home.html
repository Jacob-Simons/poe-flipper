Poeflip is a market analysis tool for Path of Exile to find the most profitable items to flip at any time. Currently only consumables are tracked for flipping as they are the easiest and most consitent way to make currency flipping. (This product isn't affiliated with or endorsed by Grinding Gear Games in any way)

Terminology:

Average Price

Average expected price to buy the item one at a time, pulled directly from poe.ninja.

Bulk Quant

Total amount of items required to bulk sell for 1ex

Profit

Total amount of profit made from sell a set for 1ex

Profit Per

Profit made per item sold in a bulk set

Supply

Total amount of items listed in the past 24hrs, pulled directly from poe.watch
